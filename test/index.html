Fire Island

Fire Island is know for it's distinctive wind mills and unique and distinct shape

This Island's name has been changed multiple times. From Currant Island to Turnagain Arm in 1794 and many more to know what it's known as Fire Island. A name established in 1895.

From Kincaid Park we trekked the 5.6km path to the Island. Fortunately, when we arrived at the Island it was during low- tide. We were able to cross to the Island.

After a short time the tide began to rise. We promtly crossed back to the main land so as to not get stuck on the Island unable to cross.
